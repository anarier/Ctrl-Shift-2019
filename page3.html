Pennsylvania Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCA)

What is the SPCA? What is their purpose? What is their mission?

The Pennsylvania Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals otherwise known as the SPCA.The SPCA is a non-profit organisation that rescues animals from inhumane conditions and abusive homes. They also try to find forever homes for the animals. They also are a non-kill coalition, which means that they don't kill their rescues.

Do they give their rescues for up for adoption? What do I need to bring with me for the adoption process if they do?

Good news, Yes they do give their rescues up for adoptoin! The only things you need to bring in order to adopt an animal are the following:

  • Dog adoption form

  • Cat adoption aplication.

    • If you have any questions please feel free to contact adoptions@pspca.org.
