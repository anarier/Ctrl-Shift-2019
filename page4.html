Blood Bank of Delmarva

Information

Blood Bank of Delmarva is a non-profit community service organization provides blood and blood products to 19 on the Delmarva Penninsula. This area needs about 79,000 blood donations each year for about 20,000 patients, and as a member of America's Blood Centers, they work on helping with blood donations to their community.


Mission

Blood Bank of Delmarva's purpose is to serve the community's patients, hospitals and members by supplying safe, high quality products and related services. The Blood Bank of Delmarva lives by the core values of service, teamwork, accountability, respect, integrity and quality to ensure efficiency in their work.

Central Pennsylvania Blood Bank


Mission

Central Pennsylvania is a non-profit community based blood program whose mission is to provide quality blood supply ready for human need. They supply blood to a multitude of websites in PA, including Carlisle Regional Medical Center. They are also part of America's Blood Centers.
